# Code of Conduct

The openMAG Project is committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive, and professional community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Focus on evidence and reproducibility
- Keep discussions professional and on-topic

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hostility
- Sharing proprietary or illegally obtained materials
- Encouraging unsafe or unlawful behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation
if behavior does not align with these guidelines.
